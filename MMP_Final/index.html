Who We Are...

The Queens Collective is a non-profit organization dedicated to showcasing the best concerts, galleries, and more in Queens, New York.
Starting in 2017, our website is you're one stop database to all things Queens. Check out our blog tab for reviews on venues and bars.
If you have an event coming up and would like us to feature it, feel free to contact us!
